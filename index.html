FAQ

What should I bring?
At the very minmum a laptop, a charger, and a small bag for swag (and hopefully your uncompressed soul)

Where does the name come from? PiperCon is an ode to Pied Piper from the fictional show Silicon Valley. We are not affiated with HBO or the show in any way.

How is this a pilgirmage?
It really isn't (the true pilgrimages are hs hacakthons!) The tagline won in a poll because people liked it.

Is this free?
Thanks to our partners who make this event possible, this really is free!

Will there be travel reimbursements? Unfortunately, we are not offering travel reimbursements at this time. (Unless if a sponsor wants to pay everyone's $5.50 MetroCard fare!)

Where can I download more RAM? Sure! A video with instructions can be found by clicking here.

How can I contact the organizers? Email hello at victorlourng dot com and we'll get back to you ASAP.
